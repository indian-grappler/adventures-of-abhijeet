This site is having all the stories listed by me - Abhijeet Singh Jadeja. These stories are previously published mainly on bearhugger.net by my then pen name 'Samrat'. 'Abhijeet Singh Jadeja' is not my real name either, but I have kind of attraction with that name and surname. Name is more meaningful to my adventures - Abhijeet means 'victorious' or 'conqueror'. While the surname 'Jadeja' is lead surname of fighter clans having some great fighters in history, as well as the surname of noticeable royal families in my home state.
